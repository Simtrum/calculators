Time Bandwidth Product


The following are some calculator that will help you calculate the Time Bandwidth Product ,by inputting the value of Central wavelength (in nm), Half Width of Laser Spectrum (in nm) and Half Width of Laser Pulse (in fs).


The formula that will be used for Time Bandwidth Product will be as followed:

$TBP$ = $\frac{c \triangle \lambda \tau}{\lambda_0^2}$


Pulse Shape, A; Center wavelength, $\lambda_0$; Speed of Light, C;
Half Width of Laser Spectrum, $\triangle \lambda$;

Input
Laser Center wavelength, $\lambda_0$:
nm
Half Width of Laser Spectrum, $\triangle \lambda$:
nm

Half Width of Laser Pulse:
fs


Output
Time Bandwidth Product:

Pulse Shape:

Gaussian: 0.441
Sech: 0.315
Lorentz: 0.142
