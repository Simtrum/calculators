Peak Power of Pulses


The following are some calculator that will help you calculate the Peak Power for Gaussian Pulse and the Peak Power for Sech Pulse (in Watts) by inputting the value of Pulse Energy (in Joules) and Pulse Duration (in Seconds).


Input
Pulse Energy, $E$:
J
Pusle Duration, $\triangle t$:
S


Output
Peak Power For Gaussian Pulse:
W

Peak Power For Sech Pulse:
W

Peak Power For Gaussian Pulse

The formula that will be used for Peak Power for Gaussian Pulse is as followed:


$P_{Gaussian}$ = $(\frac{0.94E}{\triangle t})$

Peak Power For Sech Pulse

The formula that will be used for Peak Power for Sech Pulse is as followed:


$P_{Sech}$ = $(\frac{0.88E}{\triangle t})$
