Peak Intensity of Gaussian Laser Pulses


The following are some calculator that will help you calculate the Energy fluence (in Joules per centimetre square) and the Peak Intensity (in Watts per centimetre square)

by inputting the value of Pulse Energy (in Joules) ,Beam radium at $\frac{1}{e^2}$ intensity (in centimetre) and Pulse duration (in second).

The formula that will be used are is as followed:

Peak Intensity = $(\frac{1.88E}{\pi\omega^2\tau})$

Energy fluence = $(\frac{2E}{\pi\omega^2})$
