Laser Beam Spot Size Calculator


This calculator will help you calculate the Beam diameter at focus, by inputting the $\frac{1}{e^2}$ spot diameter of the collimated Gaussian beam whose wavelength is $\lambda$. Together with the diameter of lens $D$ and the focal length $f$. In addition, by adding in the beam quality parameter $M^2$ into the formula will makes the output value more accurate.

${2w_0}$= $\frac{4M^2 \lambda f }{\pi D}$
