Round Trip Time And Repetition Frequency Of A Pulsed Laser Cavity


This calcaulator will help you computes the round trip time and repetition frequency of a pulsed laser cavity.

The formulas that will be used are as followed:

$C$=3x10$^8$

$OPL$=2L$_1$n$_{g^1}$+2L$_2$n$_{g^2}$+L$_3$n$_{g^3}$+L$_4$n$_{g^4}$

$\tau$ = $\frac{OPL}{C}$

$f_{rep}$= $\frac{1}{\tau}$

Material 1 (or2) length with double pass, L$_1$ (L$_2$);

Material 3 (or4) length with single pass, L$_3$ (L$_4$);

Material 1 (2,3,4) group index, $n_{g^1} (n_{g^2} n_{g^3} n_{g^4})$;
