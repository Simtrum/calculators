Mode Field Diameter Calculator


This calculator will help you computes the Mode-field diameter of the fundamental mode in a step-index fiber.

The formula that will be used is as followed:

$M F D$= $2a(0.65 + \frac{1.619}{V^{3/2}}+\frac{2.879}{V^6})$

$V$= $\frac{2 \pi}{\lambda} a$NA
