Gaussian beam propagation-divergence


The following is a calculator that helps you calculate the Divergence angle $\theta$, by inputting the Beam waist, Beam quality factor and Central wavelength.

The formula that will be used is as followed:

${\theta}= 2M^2 (\frac{λ}{\pi w_0})$
